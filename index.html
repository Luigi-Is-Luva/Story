Running Late

By Luigi Dominguez

A little animated story about a simple guy ruinning late.

In a place far away, there was a student who forgot to turn on his alarm .

He immediately ran through the woods to get to school

He got to the train station and took the train

When he got to the campus he realized it was saturday!!
